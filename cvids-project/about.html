How we started

Early in 2006, a group of people were brought together by Father Luciano Cortopassi (at the time, Fr. Luciano was pastor of St. Maria Goretti Italian Parish in Edmonton) with the purpose of providing humanitarian aid to the poor – particularly children – of developing countries.
